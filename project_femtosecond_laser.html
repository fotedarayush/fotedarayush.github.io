Femtosecond Laser Micromachining (FIB-SEM)

Overview

In-situ femtosecond-laser micromachining integrated with a FIB-SEM workflow. Investigated trench depth vs hatches/power, minimal thermal effects, and EBSD/SEM analysis.

Setup & Methods

  • Zeiss Crossbeam 350 FIB-SEM + fs-laser for rapid, clean access to buried ROIs.
  • Laser “recipes” for aluminium & steel; ablation threshold estimation; self-focusing considerations.
  • EBSD/EDS/SE/BSE/CL measurements for surface & compositional analysis.
