Estimating the Hubble Constant via Gravitational Waves

This project involved developing a Python-based simulation to estimate the Hubble constant using gravitational wave signals from binary black hole mergers (dark sirens)...

  • Used truncated Gaussian and Fisher distributions to simulate galaxy catalogs.
  • Ran Monte Carlo simulations to estimate likelihood over a range of $H_0$ values.
  • Studied host galaxy identification through probabilistic matching.
