Hello, I'm Ayush

I am an early-career theoretical physicist with an MPhys (Integrated Masters) in Physics & Astrophysics from the University of Leicester. My interests centre on string theory and physical mathematics, quantum field theory, general relativity, and cosmology. For my specialist project, I built a Python-based dark standard siren simulation and, in a fully Bayesian framework, recovered H_0 ≈ 72 km s⁻¹ Mpc⁻¹ from simulated gravitational wave events. I aim for mathematical rigour, symmetry principles, Lagrangian/Hamiltonian methods, and differential geometry. I also led student societies (President, Physics Society; Vice-President, AstRoSoc), organising workshops and trips. I’m now seeking to work on moduli spaces, quantum corrections, sigma models, and dualities.
